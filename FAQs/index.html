Frequently Asked Questions

What is J-POS?

J-POS is a Java-based Point of Sales System designed for small businesses. It helps manage sales, inventory, and customers, providing a simple and efficient way to run your business.

How can I get started with J-POS?

Getting started with J-POS is easy! Simply visit our website and sign up for an account. Once you've signed up, you can log in and start using the system right away.

Can I track my inventory in real-time with J-POS?

Yes, absolutely! J-POS allows you to track your inventory in real-time. You can view stock levels, get alerts for low stock items, and easily manage your inventory to avoid stockouts.
