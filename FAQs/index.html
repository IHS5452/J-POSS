Frequently Asked Questions

What is J-POSS?

J-POSS is a Java-based Point of Sales System designed for small businesses. It is created by HeartStone Virtual Solutions, LLC. It helps manage sales, inventory, and customers, providing a simple and efficient way to run any type of small business.

How can I get started with J-POSS?

Getting started with J-POSS is easy! Once you decide that J-POSS is right for you via our free demo, purchase a product key and download the installer. Once you have J-POSS installed onto all your systems, have management head to the admin portal to create accounts, import products, and more!

Hpw much is a product key?

Prices for product keys are based on how many machines J-POSS will be installed on. They start at $13 for a single license key, $60 for 5 license keys, and, $119 for 10 license keys. If you need more than 10, please contact us
